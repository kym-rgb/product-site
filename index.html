FAQs

Common onboarding questions, answered.

How do I enable or disable the AI chatbot?

Go to Settings > AI Chatbot Beta > Feature Toggle. Turn it on or off anytime for the creator list you provided.

Can I enable the AI chatbot for my male or transexual creators?

No. At this stage we only support English-speaking female creators. Support for additional creator profiles is planned for later releases.

How can I know when AI is handling a chat?

Yes. In Message Pro, AI-handled chats are clearly marked with icons and banners, so your team can easily tell when a conversation is being managed by AI versus a human.

How can I see the chats that are being handled by the chatbot?

By default, AI-handled chats are hidden in the inbox. Find the filter in Message Pro, toggle it off to see all chats, including those managed by AI.

How do chatters know when they need to step in for an AI-handled chat?

By default, AI-handled chats are hidden in Message Pro. When a fan triggers exit rules and needs human attention, the chat reappears in Message Pro with an unread red dot so your chatter knows to step in.

How can I stop AI for a specific fan?

Inside a chat, click the Exit button to terminate AI instantly for that fan.

How do I prepare media for the AI chatbot?

Create or designate a folder in Vault that contains all the media the chatbot can use. The bot will only draw from that folder.

Can the bot automatically send free teasers?

Yes. Just place your free teaser files in the designated AI folder. Leave the suggested price field empty ‚Äî that way the system knows it‚Äôs a free teaser, not PPV content.

Can I set up bundles?

Yes. Add a note to each file like ‚Äúbundle 1‚Äù (or 2, 3, etc.) so we know which media belongs together. You can create as many bundles as you need.

Where do I find the fan summary?

Fan summaries appear in Message Pro > Fan Insight > Fan Note. If the fan subscribes to the main page, it syncs automatically to the main account too.
